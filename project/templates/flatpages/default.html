About Gestalt Psychology

Gestalt psychology focuses on understanding the human mind and behavior as a whole, emphasizing that the whole is greater than the sum of its parts. It highlights the human ability to perceive patterns and organized structures.

Core Principles

Figure-Ground

Distinguish an object from its background.

Proximity

Group objects based on their closeness.

Similarity

Perceive similar objects as a unit.

Closure

Mentally fill in gaps to see complete shapes.

Continuity

Prefer continuous patterns and lines.
